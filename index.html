Instructions

At the beginning of each game, you'll be given a random target number to hit.

There are four different crystals/characters below. By clicking on one of the crystals/characters, you will add points to your score

To win the game, you must match the score exactly. Go over, and you'll lose.

The value of each crystal/character will be hidden at the start of each game until you click on it.

These values will change at the start of each new game.
